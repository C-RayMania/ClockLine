Everything New in Chapter 3 Season 4:

  • Chrome Splash: Allows you to phase through walls and builds.

  • Become a Blob of Chrome by throwing down a Chrome Splash which makes you immune to fire and fall damage and gives the ability to dash mid-air and phase through any walls.

  • D-Launcher: Helps gain higher ground by seeking "safety" in the POIs which have been given hot air ballons to float in air.

  • EvoChrome Weapons: The EvoChrome Shotgun and EvoChrome Burst Rifle, obtained from Chrome Chests, increase in rarity the more one damages opponents with them. Can go from Uncommon to Mythic
